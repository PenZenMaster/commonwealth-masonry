About Us

Commonwealth Masonry & Paving

Get To Know More About Us! You can rely on Commonwealth Masonry & Paving for all of your concrete, natural stone, waterproofing, brick, drains, and paving needs, as we are a reputable and established company in the construction industry. Exceptional craftsmanship and individualized service are priorities for our team of highly trained experts. We are Boston’s third-generation masons. Years of experience made us the go-to construction company for both commercial and private buildings. Since we pay close attention to detail, only use high-quality materials, and follow all relevant protocols, such as obtaining the necessary licenses and insurance, our finished products withstand the test of time. We can handle any installation or repair job, no matter how big or small. Commonwealth Masonry & Paving places a premium on 100% customer satisfaction and constantly strives to go above and beyond for all our clients. Our team works with you hand in hand to make your dreams realized within the constraints of your specified time frame and budget. For us, the key to a successful business relationship is establishing a foundation of trust with our customers and keeping an open line of communication with them over time. Commonwealth Masonry & Paving is the company to call if you need a skilled and dependable contractor for your next building endeavor. Paving, concrete, brickwork, basement drainage and waterproofing are just some of our services. Our professionals use only the finest materials and modern tools and equipment to ensure your project is completed to the highest standards. Because as we say, quality isn’t expensive, it’s priceless!
